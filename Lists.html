List of US States

  1. Alabama

    2. Alabama is a state located in the southeastern region of the United States. It is bordered by Tennessee to the north, Georgia to the east, Florida to the south, and Mississippi to the west.   2. Alaska

    3. Alaska is a state located in the northwest extremity of North America. It is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, Canada to the east, the Pacific Ocean to the south, and the Bering Sea to the west. Alaska is the largest state in the United States by area, and it has a diverse geography that includes mountains, forests, and tundra.

  3. Arizona

    4. Arizona is a state located in the southwestern region of the United States. It is bordered by California to the west, Nevada to the northwest, Utah to the north, Colorado to the northeast, New Mexico to the east, and Mexico to the south. Arizona is known for its desert climate, the Grand Canyon, and its Native American history.

  4. Arkansas

    5. Arkansas is a state located in the southern region of the United States. It is bordered by Missouri to the north, Tennessee to the northeast, Mississippi to the east, Louisiana to the south, Texas to the southwest, and Oklahoma to the west. Arkansas is known for its natural beauty, including the Ozark Mountains and Hot Springs National Park.

  5. California

    6. California is a state located on the west coast of the United States. It is bordered by Oregon to the north, Nevada to the east, Arizona to the southeast, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. California is known for its diverse geography, including beaches, mountains, and deserts, as well as its cultural and economic significance.
